a sketchbook

Here you will find small ideas, project sketches, or other incomplete thoughts that I've found interesting at some point.

  • Meditations
    An experiment in adding interactivity and build-up to the standard D3 force layout. Why does every D3 force layout example show a fixed number of nodes? A network should be revealed as it explored, and here we meditate on phrases as our network of hopes and ideals is revealed.
  • 2014 Oakland Mayoral Campaign Financial Summaries
    A hackathon project from Oakland Data Day on how to visualize campaign finance public records, in the context of the 2014 Oakland Mayoral race.
